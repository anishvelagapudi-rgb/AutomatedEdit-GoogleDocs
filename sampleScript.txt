Hello, welcome to this demo of human-like typing automation!

This script simulates typing in a Google Doc with random pauses and breaks. The goal is to mimic human behavior by typing out text at a natural speed, taking breaks, and creating pauses between words and sentences.

Here’s how it works:
1. The script opens a Google Doc.
2. It types out each word one by one, with random pauses between each character.
3. After a few minutes of typing, it takes a break, just like a real person would.
4. Once the break is over, it continues typing where it left off.

You can customize the time intervals, the text file input, and the Google Docs link as per your needs.

Hope this demo helps you understand how this can be applied to other typing tasks!

Thanks for trying it out!
